Born in Reading, United Kingdom, in 1993, Grace Jabbari is a professional dancer, the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Jabbari. Her unique name reflects her diverse heritage, with an Iranian father and a British mother.

Grace Jabbari, a Reading native, underwent training at Tring Park School for Performing Arts. She earned a first-class BA from Rambert School and an MA from the Northern School of Contemporary Dance. Post-graduation, Jabbari‘s career unfolded with notable achievements.
