Pam Byse-Chestnut is the beautiful wife of actor Morris Chestnut.

The couple has stayed married since 1995, and together they have a son and a daughter.

About Morris Chestnut

Morris Chestnut has had a multifaceted career as an actor.

Chestnut’s breakthrough came in 1991 with his role as Ricky in the impactful film “Boyz n the Hood,” catapulting him to prominence.

Since then, he has graced both the big and small screens with his compelling performances.

One notable collaboration in Chestnut’s career involves working with action star Steven Seagal. Their dynamic partnership unfolded in films like “Under Siege 2: Dark Territory,” where Chestnut portrayed Seagal’s reluctant partner.

The duo reunited in “Half Past Dead” and “Prince of Pistols,” showcasing the actor’s versatility in embodying diverse roles.

Television has also been a canvas for Chestnut’s talent. His portrayal of FBI Agent Will Keaton in the NBC series “The Enemy Within” stands out as a proof of his ability to command the screen in a leading role.

The actor’s foray into the medical drama genre saw him joining the cast of “Nurse Jackie” in 2013, portraying war veteran Dr. Ike Prentiss.

The role earned him the prestigious NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series in 2014.

In the cinematic realm, Chestnut’s repertoire continued to expand. In 2015, he graced the screen as Derrick Dog Prince in the movie “Heist,” showcasing his enduring appeal to audiences.

In 2015, Chestnut was listed amongst the “Sexiest Men Alive” by People magazine.p

In 2022, Chestnut was honored with induction into the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
