The Meaning Behind The Song: Tell It by Walmart Associate Choir

July 25, 2024

As a longtime Walmart associate, I have had the pleasure of being a part of the Walmart Associate Choir and performing the powerful song “Tell It.” This song holds a special place in my heart, as it embodies the spirit of unity, empowerment, and the joy of community that we experience as Walmart associates.

A Call to Action

The lyrics of “Tell It” carry a powerful message that resonates with everyone who hears it. The song begins with the powerful line, “Can you feel the rhythm, it’s coming from your soul.” This serves as a reminder to listen to our hearts, embrace our passions, and recognize the power within us to make a difference.

The chorus of the song urges us to take action and share our stories: “We’re gonna jump, shout, tell ’em all about it. If you want the world to see, you can’t make waves just standing still. You gotta make a little noise, you gotta rattle the tree.” These lines remind us that it is not enough to stay silent. We must raise our voices, make some noise, and share our experiences in order to create positive change and spread awareness.

Inspiring Unity and Community

“Tell It” also emphasizes the importance of unity and community. The lyrics describe us as “one big family” and convey the idea that by working together, we can achieve incredible things. It encourages us to reach for the sky and change the world together.

Furthermore, the verse sung by the male singer and female singer highlights the call to action as a shared responsibility: “Others want to follow, if only you and I will move. Hear that voice calling, it’s coming from your heart. It’s gonna stay right with you until we do our part.” These lines inspire us to be catalysts for change and inspire others to join us on this journey.

A Reflection of Our Experiences

Personally, this song has touched my heart and resonates deeply with my experiences as a Walmart associate. It reminds me of the strong bond we share as a team, the passion we have for our work, and the impact we can make when we come together.

Through my years at Walmart, I have witnessed incredible stories of personal growth, camaraderie, and triumph. This song captures the essence of those stories and encourages us to share them with the world, to inspire others and drive positive change.

Conclusion

“Tell It” by the Walmart Associate Choir is more than just a song. It represents the collective power of the Walmart associates and celebrates the unity, empowerment, and community we foster within our organization. Through its uplifting lyrics, it calls us to action and encourages us to raise our voices, make a difference, and inspire others. As a proud Walmart associate, I am honored to have been a part of the Walmart Associate Choir and to share the message of “Tell It” with the world.
