Lev Cameron Khmelev is a French-American dancer, actor, and social media star, best known as the competitor on the reality-TV dancing show, Dancing with the Stars: Juniors in 2018. He also appeared on Dancing with the Stars the same year as a junior dancer and was also in the Top 30 competitors on the TV show So You Think You Can Dance: The Next Generation in 2016. As an actor, Lev has appeared in TV series such as My Haunted House (2016) as Henry, 555 (2017) as Little Boy, Evil Things (2017) as Harris, and played Squirgly Dag in the movie Ham on Rye (2019).
