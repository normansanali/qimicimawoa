Jake Funk Parents

Jake Funk’s parents Jim and A’Lisa Funk are stars of their universe. One’s a footballer and the other’s a phenomenal swimmer. No wonder, he inherited the talent for athleticism from them.

Guess, Jake has a lot to thank them for.

Jake Funk Dad: Jim Funk

Meaning, he had Jake while he was 38.

Jim Funk Mom: A’Lisa Funk

  • A’Lisa Funk Age

A’Lisa Funk was born on December 31, 1963, making her 58 years of age in 2022.

Also, she was 35 years of age when she had Jake.

  • A’Lisa Funk Job

A’Lisa Funk is a retired competitive swimmer who has several accolades to her name. Listing some of them are — 3-time Division II National Team Title winner, 22-time All-American, and 5-time individual NCAA Relay National Champion.

Also, she was a member of the Clarion Athletics Hall of Fame during her time at Clarion University.

Talking more about her achievements, A’Lisa (a freestyle/butterfly specialist) was one of the main contributors to the Clarion Women’s Swimming and Diving team that dominated the PSAC and Division II from 1983-86. As a co-captain, A’Lisa led Clarion to D-II and PSAC, and also went on to become a 14-time PSAC Champion taking 4 individual titles and 10 relays in 1983.

After retiring as a swimmer, she went on to take a job at Booz Allen Hamilton in Virginia and settled with her husband and kids.
