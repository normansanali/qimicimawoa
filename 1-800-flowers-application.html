Facts About Working At 1-800-Flowers

It is necessary to be at least eighteen years old to work at 1-800-Flowers. The company’s stores typically operate daily from 9 am to 8 pm during the week and 5 pm on weekends. However, at certain times of the year, some areas of the company operate around the clock to assist customers.

The company is especially busy during special holidays such as Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, and Christmas. Therefore, additional employees are often hired just before these holidays to help deal with the demand. This is a good opportunity for students to gain a little work experience and discover if this job is for them.

1-800-Flowers Employment Opportunities

A wide range of entry-level employees are regularly employed at 1-800-Flowers to keep up with increasing demands. There are also roles for experienced professionals in areas such as marketing, administration, and management. Let’s take a look at some of the most popular jobs at 1-800-Flowers and the duties that go with them.

Customer Service Representative

These employees usually work in a customer service center or from a home office. They are responsible for receiving calls from customers, answering questions, and resolving issues. They need to have deep knowledge of 1-800-Flowers products and services and excellent customer service skills.

This role is typically open to entry-level candidates, and paid training is available. Employees can select either part-time or full-time hours. On average, customer service representatives make $9 per hour.

Florist

These employees grow and arrange the flowers that are sold by 1-800-Flowers. Florists typically work in a 1-800-Flowers retail store and cut and package flowers on demand. They interact with customers and make recommendations based on budget and preferences.

This role is open to anyone who holds a high school diploma and has a good knowledge of flowers. The starting salary for a florist at 1-800-Flowers is usually $10 per hour.

Retail Sales Associate

This role combined customer service and sales skills. Retail sales associates work closely with florists and help customers find what they need. They help to keep the retail store clean and tidy and typically make $10 per hour.

Merchandise Coordinator

This is an office job that requires good attention to detail and the ability to work independently. Merchandise coordinators manage the product setup process and perform data entry tasks. This role requires good knowledge of spreadsheets and computer programs and pays around $65,000 per year.

Retail Store Manager

These professional full-time employees are in charge of a single 1-800-Flowers retail store. They are responsible for hiring and training staff and assisting them with their duties when necessary. Retail store managers are also charged with making sure that the store meets the set sales targets throughout the year.

Retail store managers also have to do regular inventory checks and order new products when necessary. They liaise with customers, warehouse employees, and a range of other people to ensure smooth operations. The average salary for a retail store manager is $75,000 per year.

Additional Benefits of Working at 1-800-Flowers

Dedicated 1-800-Flowers employees of all levels are provided with flexible scheduling options and advancement opportunities. Eligible full-time employees are also treated to a wide range of other company benefits, including paid time off. Other benefits include vacation days, performance-based bonuses, and access to retirement plans.

How To Apply?

The best way to secure a job at 1-800-Flowers is to visit the careers section of the company’s website. A wide range of different vacancies are presented, and the list is regularly updated. It is possible to narrow the search by choosing a specific location or searching by job title.

Clicking on a job title from the list will reveal the full job description, which includes the hiring requirements. After reading the description carefully, candidates can click on the apply button at the bottom of the screen. This will direct candidates to the 1-800-Flowers application online.
