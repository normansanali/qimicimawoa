Billy Ray Cyrus is an American country musician and actor who was born on August 25, 1961. Since 1992, he has released 16 studio albums and 53 singles.

His popular song “Achy Breaky Heart” peaked at number one on the U.S. Hot Country Songs list and became the first song to ever be certified triple platinum in Australia. It also had the most single sales in the same nation in 1992. As a result of the song’s music video, the line dance became more well-known.

Billy Ray Cyrus house: Where does Billy Ray Cyrus live?

Billy Ray Cyrus resides in Nashville, Tennessee, the birthplace of country music. The 1,200 square foot house was purchased by Billy and his wife Tish.

It has grown to 3,000 square feet and five bedrooms. Since then, Tish has completely renovated the house, transforming it into a lovely, laid-back, modern, yet endearing family home.
