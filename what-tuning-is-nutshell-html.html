In order to follow along with the lesson and the original audio, you will need to tune your guitar down one-half step on your guitar. Those notes are Eb Ab Db Gb Bb Eb, commencing with the 6th string and progressing through the rest of the strings.

When it comes to this, what key does Nutshell occupy?

It is also possible to inquire as to what tuning Alice in Chains employs.

Cantrell’s primary Rampage is tuned to what he refers to as “normal Alice in Chains tuning,” which is standard tuning one half step lower from standard tuning in Alice in Chains (Eb Ab Db Gb Bb Eb). A second Rampage, which he purchased shortly after acquiring the first, is tuned one half step lower than the first (Db Ab Db Gb Bb Eb).

So, how do you go about reading guitar tabs while keeping this in mind?

TAB is made up of six horizontal lines, which correspond to the six strings on a guitar. The top line represents the first string, which is the thinnest, and the bottom line represents the sixth string, which is the thickest. The numbers that have been set on the lines tell you what fret to use to play a note on the instrument.
