Womens WarGames Match Set For WWE Survivor Series

August 11, 2024
blog

Another match has been added to the card for WWE’s Survivor Series: WarGames, in the form of a big Women’s WarGames match.

During tonight’s episode of WWE SmackDown, things opened up with a segment featuring Damage CTRL. In the ring, Bayley gloated about her plan to bring Asuka into the fold, and promptly welcomed her into the group with a t-shirt.

However, things were short-lived as Dakota Kai revealed that IYO SKY wanted to challenge Bianca Belair and co. to a WarGames match. Things broke down shortly after when Shotzi appeared alongside Belair and Charlotte Flair to clear the ring and freak out the group.

The match itself would be Damage CTRL’s Asuka, Bayley, Kairi Sane, IYO SKY, and Kairi Sane against Belair, Flair, Shotzi, and a superstar to be named later.

The full card for Survivor Series: WarGames is as follows:

  • : Gunther (c) vs. The Miz
  • : Rhea Ripley (c) vs. Zoey Stark
  • : Cody Rhodes, Seth “Freakin” Rollins, Jey Uso, and Sami Zayn vs. The Judgment Day (Damian Priest, Finn Bálor, “Dirty” Dominik Mysterio, and JD McDonagh)
  • : Bianca Belair, Charlotte Flair, Shotzi, and TBA vs. Damage CTRL (Bayley, Asuka, Iyo Sky, and Kairi Sane)
