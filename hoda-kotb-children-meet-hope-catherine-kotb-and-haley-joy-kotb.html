Hoda Kotb is an Egyptian-American broadcast journalist, television personality, and author who is well known as the main co-anchor of the NBC News morning show Today and co-host of its entertainment-focused fourth hour.

In this Article, we will talk about Hoda Kotb children and other aspects of her.

Who is Hoda Kotb ?

Hoda Kotb was born on August 9, 1964 in Norman, a suburb of Oklahoma, United States where she grew up under the care of her parents. She is 58 years of age.

She was born to Egyptian parents, Her father is Abdel Kader Kotb who was  a fossil energy specialist. Her mother is called Sameha. Hoda’s father died at the age of 51 in 1986.

Hoda attended the Fort Hunt High School and graduated in 1982. In 1986, Hoda Kotb graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in broadcast journalism. 

She quickly established herself as one of the top Broadcast journalists having formerly served as a correspondent for the television news magazine program Dateline NBC.

Hoda Kotb Children

Hoda Kotb hasn’t given birth herself, she however has two adopted children. She had adopted a baby girl named Haley Joy Kotb in February 2017. On April 16 2019, she announced that she adopted a second baby girl named Hope Catherine Kotb.
