Phenomenon on both YouTube and Instagram who has earned more than 5 million subscribers for his Soy Francisco ALV YouTube channel. He is known for his dad vlogs, comedy content, fact videos and more.

Before Fame

He debuted on YouTube in November of 2017 with a video called "MI PRIMER VIDEO."

Trivia

He has had the members of the YouTube group JukiLop on his channel. He has also earned more than 3.1 million followers on Instagram.

Family Life

His full name is Francisco Eduardo Alvarado Ríos. He was born in Hermosillo, Sonora but grew up in Mazatlán, Sinaloa. He has three older brothers. He became a stepfather at the age of 17 to his girlfriend Sofía Salas' daughter, Hannah, from a previous relationship. 

Associated With
