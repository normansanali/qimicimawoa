Who is David Vogel from The Voice?

The Voice David Vogel is a lead singer for the band JADN. They have 23.8k monthly listeners on Spotify till now.

The band first single to release on Spotify in 2019 was Little Love from the album City Skies. David was the one who wrote the title track City Skies in 2017.

He often travels with his music band all throughout NYC. But his plans to tour outside the city were postponed due to the ongoing pandemic.

The New York-based band has four other members, including his brother Jeremy Vogel as the guitarist and Nick Gamble. Matt Kalapuch is the drummer, whereas it has Aaron Bishop as bassist.

The band name JADN comes from the initials of the first name of members. Besides, Matt joined after the band was created. Now, it’s no longer a mystery to us.

During the lockdown, the members of the boy group engaged themselves in writing and creating songs. They finally released their first EP A futile Attempt.

JADN’s first four-song EP is already on Apple Music and other music-sharing platforms. The band characterized themselves as fusing the elements of rock, pop, and 80’s to create something fresh and familiar for their listeners.
