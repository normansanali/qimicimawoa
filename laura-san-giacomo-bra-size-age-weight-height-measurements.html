Biography:

Laura San Giacomo was born on November 14, 1962 in West Orange, New Jersey, USA. She attended Carnegie Mellon School of Drama in Pittsburgh where she received a Fine Arts degree, specializing in acting.

She then appeared on television with notable roles in Crime Story and Miami Vice. In 1989, she made her breakthrough with the film Sex, Lies, and Videotape.
