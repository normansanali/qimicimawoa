Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck Are All Smiles As They Reunite in L.A.

September 09, 2024
blog

All is well for exes Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck. The actors and co-parents were all smiles as they stepped out together in Los Angeles this week.

For the Santa Monica outing, Garner looked casual in a white tee, classic blue jeans, a navy blue cropped rain jacket, and square black shades. She wore her hair in a straight bob and held a small coffee in her hand as she walked to her car with Affleck.

The Air star also kept it cool and casual in a black T-shirt, beige khakis, and a blue puffer jacket. He carried a black backpack.

x17online

Garner and Affleck married in 2005 and welcomed three children together, Violet Anne, 17, Seraphina Rose, 14 and Samuel, 11. They split in 2015 and finalized their divorce in October 2018.

The exes keep their family lives private, but they have been pictured reuniting several times since their separation, both alone and with their kids. On one occasion, in April, Affleck’s new wife, Jennifer Lopez, joined him and Garner for an outing with their kids. And in May, they all got together to support Seraphina in a school play.

(Lopez is mom to twins Max and Emme, 15, from her marriage to Marc Anthony.)

Affleck and Lopez rekindled their romance in 2021—decades after their initial relationship and engagement in 2002—and married in a lavish ceremony on July 2022. They now live together with their blended family.
