15 Things About The Gellers That Make Zero Sense

September 04, 2024
blog

Jack Geller, Judy Geller, Ross Geller, and Monica Geller make up one of the weirdest and funniest families on TV. Friends was a hit TV show in the 90s that was focused on a group of six adults in their late 20s, trying to navigate through life in New York City when it came to dating, friendship, employment, and more. The Geller family added a lot of interesting moments to the show.

If it wasn’t for the hilarious Geller parents on friends, we wouldn’t understand why Ross and Monica turned out the way that they did. There are a few things about this family unit they don’t really make a lot of sense. For example, the fact that Monica dated one of her dad’s best friends is super weird. Most families cannot relate to a situation as awkward as that. Continue reading to find out everything else that doesn’t make sense about this family.

15 Would Jack And Judy Make It So Obvious That They Favor Ross Over Monica IRL?

It’s common knowledge that parents are not supposed to show favoritism between their kids! That’s why it’s so weird and interesting that Jack and Judy Geller made it obvious that they prefer Ross over Monica. For example, they accepted all of the women that Ross chooses to date but always criticized the men Monica dates.

14 Ross Claimed To Not Like Ice Cream But He's Seen Eating It Later On With Marcel

Ross makes the claim that he doesn’t like eating ice cream because it’s too cold for his teeth but later on, we end up seeing him eating ice cream! Our question is… Does he really get toothaches from eating ice cream or was he just saying that? The writers clearly slipped up.

13 Monica Actually Opened Her Wedding Gifts Without Chandler?

When a couple gets married, one of the most exciting things for them to do, aside from the actual wedding ceremony, is to open their gifts together! That is why it is pretty shocking that Monika decided to open her wedding gifts without Chandler by her side. This is something they should’ve done together.

12 Ross Said He'd Only Been With Carol But He Also Hooked Up With A Housekeeper In College

Ross told everyone that Carol was the first woman he’s been with, but in a liter anecdote, it was revealed that he hooked up with a housekeeper that cleaned the college dorms where he lived. It is also revealed that he was drunk during that time, so maybe he just didn’t remember hooking up with her.

11 Monica Would NEVER Have A Secret Clutter Closet

Monica is a neat-freak who cares about keeping everything as tidy as possible, no matter what. She does whatever it takes to make sure that her apartment is always perfectly organized. That is why it shocks us that she has an entire closet dedicated to clutter! It’s so unlike her!

10 Ross Lied About Getting An Annulment From Rachel... Who Does That?!

Ross and Rachel had a very complicated relationship but the good part about it at all is that they ended up happy together by the time the show came to an end. It is pretty strange that Ross decided to lie about getting an annulment from Rachel after their Vegas wedding. This isn’t something to lie about.

9 Judy Constantly Body Shames Monica

Judy Geller is known for body-shaming Monica and making Monica feel like she wasn’t the most amazing daughter ever. Good moms always remind their daughters that their daughters are beautiful. They compliment their daughters on the positives. Judy Geller failed at that.

8 What Happened To Ross' Son, Ben?

At the beginning of the show, we see Ross spending time with the son he shares with Carol. By the end of the show, we never see Ross spending any time with his son anymore! He was a devoted father at the beginning so it’s very strange that he totally changed his ways.

7 Monica Wanted To Spend Chandler's Savings On Their Wedding

Monica was okay with the notion of spending all of Chandler’s savings on their wedding. The wedding is only one day out of an entire lifetime so the fact that she was so pushy towards the idea of him spending his savings on the wedding is kind of messed up. She shouldn’t have pressured that.

6 Ross Was 29 Years Old For Three Season

For some reason, Ross was 29 years old for three seasons in a row. He mentioned the fact that he was 29 multiple times and it caused quite a bit of confusion for viewers. We are wondering just how old Ross really was? There is no way that he remained 29 for that long!

5 Monica Prefers Fun Bobby When He's Under The Influence

Monica used to date Fun Bobby when he was a party guy who liked drinking but when she reconnected with him later on, she didn’t like him anymore due to his sobriety. Monica seems like the type of woman who would appreciate a man making positive changes for his health and lifestyle.

4 Ross Broke Into Mona's Apartment... To Retrieve A Shirt!

Ross Geller did something totally inappropriate when he broke into Mona's apartment to retrieve his shirt. Getting a shirt back is not that serious and it is definitely not a valid reason to break into someone’s apartment! The fact that Ross did this is pretty messed up, to be honest.

3 Monica Flirts With Soap Opera Actors After Marrying Chandler

Monica Geller flirted with soap opera actors… After she married Chandler. This is something that makes zero sense to us. Her relationship with Chandler should have been something that she was cherishing and valuing on a higher level. That means she shouldn’t have been flirting with other people.

2 Ross Married Emily Anyway, Despite All Of Their Drama

Ross and Emily had so many problems leading up to their wedding day that it made zero sense for them to have followed through with their wedding plans anyway. It was clear to both of them that Ross had feelings for Rachel so the fact that they went through with the wedding is very odd.

1 Monica And Ross Kissed Each Other

The fact that Monica and Ross kissed each other is super creepy and super weird! The only redeeming part of the story is the fact that they didn’t know that they were kissing each other. They didn’t find out that they had kissed each other until years later when they were sharing the anecdote.
