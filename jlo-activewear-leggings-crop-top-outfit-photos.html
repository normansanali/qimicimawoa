While many people are preparing to skip the gym over the holiday season, Jennifer Lopez is making sure she continues to get her workouts in before the festive break.

The superstar stepped out on Wednesday wearing not one, but two, sleek activewear looks, proving that it's possible to look chic in the gym.

J.Lo was spotted wearing a see-through lemon crop top, which showed off her toned midriff, with cobalt blue leggings. She carried her beloved bedazzled Coach tumbler, and wore a matching glittery face mask. As per usual, Lopez was our undisputed style icon, and we only wish we looked quite as good in sportswear.

The "In the Morning" singer was also photographed wearing a head to toe blue look, consisting of skintight leggings, a matching sweatshirt, oversized gold hoop earrings, and a sparkly face mask. She also wore a white Valentino Garavani Supervee small leather shoulder bag to complete the look, which retails for $2,195.

The Hustlers star recently revealed her new skincare line, JLo Beauty, and opened up about whether she's ever had plastic surgery or Botox. J.Lo explained, per DailyMail.com, "I haven't ever had Botox to this day ... I'm not that person. I don't have anything against people doing that; it's just not my thing. I'm more about a natural approach to skincare … but I want [my products] to work."

She continued, "I want the hyaluronic acid in there. I want the things that are going to help, because I don’t want to have to go to the needles at some point. I’m not saying one day I won’t, but I haven't yet."
