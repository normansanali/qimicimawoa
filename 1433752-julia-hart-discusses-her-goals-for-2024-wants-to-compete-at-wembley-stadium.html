Julia Hart Discusses Her Goals For 2024, Wants To Compete At Wembley Stadium

August 14, 2024
blog

AEW TBS Champion Julia Hart wants to compete at Wembley Stadium.

Julia Hart had a breakout year in 2023; the House of Black member became the youngest champion in AEW history when she won the TBS Championship. at AEW Full Gear

During an AEW Worlds End post-show media scrum, WrestleZone’s Colin Tessier asked Julia Hart to name her goals for the new year.

“I think one of my big goals for myself next year is maybe to wrestle at Wembley,” Julia Hart said. “That’s up to Tony [Khan], though. I would love to wrestle at Wembley, and I’d love to keep my streak as the TBS Champion going.”

Julia Hart Is Focused On The TBS Championship

During the scrum, Julia Har was also asked whether she had ever thought about leading her own faction. She responded by making it clear that she was focused on being a champion and representing the House of Black.

“I don’t know, I think right now, I’m just focused on being a champion and doing the best I can at being a champion,” Julia Hart said. “I like to take things one day at a time. So maybe a faction is in my future. But right now, I’m focused on this [title], I’m focused on what I can do and representing the House.”

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Dynamite as it airs on January 3.
