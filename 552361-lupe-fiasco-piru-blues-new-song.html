Piru Blues

September 19, 2024
blog

Check out Piru Blues, the latest track from Lupe Fiasco, which dropped on Wednesday, December 25th, 2013. Lupe Fiasco's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Piru Blues has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work Lupe Fiasco has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what Lupe Fiasco has on deck.

Check out Lupe Fiasco on Thot 97. If you're down, make sure to visit Lupe Fiasco's profile page for the newest info, news, songs, and mixtapes.

Lupe decides to drop off a new unreleased track that did not make it onto his 2012 album Food & Liquor II: The Great American Rap Album. The track is called "Piru Blues" and is produced by DJ SimonSayz. This is a great Christmas gift for Lupe fans. Take a close listen!

Lupe's upcoming album, Tetsuo & Youth, is due out early 2014.
