Bridget Marquardt is an American television personality, model, actor, and producer who is best known for having been cast in the reality TV series The Girls Next Door, which depicted her life as one of Playboy founder Hugh Hefner’s girlfriends. Over the years, Bridget has been a part of various other shows like Curb Your Enthusiasm, Robot Chicken, Celebrity Paranormal Project, Entourage, Holly’s World, The Apprentice: Los Angeles, Phenomenon, General Hospital, Identity, The Ellen DeGeneres Show, The Tyra Banks Show, Celebrity Family Feud, Celebrity Ghost Stories, and Ghost Adventures. In addition to that, her film credits include Intolerable Cruelty, The Telling, Tomorrow’s Yesterday, Scary Movie 4, The House Bunny, Black Friday, and Indyfans and the Quest for Fortune and Glory.
