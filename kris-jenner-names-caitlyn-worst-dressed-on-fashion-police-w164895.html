Kris Jenner Names Caitlyn Worst Dressed on 'Fashion Police'

September 20, 2024
blog

Oh, the shade! On the Thursday, February, 18, episode of Fashion Police, Kris Jenner was asked to rate her famous family’s style choices at the Yeezy Season 3 fashion show. While she placed her daughters, Kim Kardashian and Kylie Jenner, on the “Best Dressed” list, she wasn’t so kind to her ex, Caitlyn Jenner.

“Best is probably a tie between Kim and Kylie,” the momager, 60, told cohosts Giuliana Rancic, Brad Goreski and Melissa Rivers. “I have to say I love the way Kylie threw the beanie on at the end, but Kim’s skirt was everything. They both looked so damn cute.”

“But my worst, I think, is going to be Caitlyn,” Kris continued. “But wait. Hold on. Before you judge me, I can’t handle the shoes with the skirt.”

While Kris wasn’t a fan of Caitlyn’s footwear, she was quick to clarify that she only gave her former spouse, 66, a hard time because she was actually jealous of the custom Yeezy x Balmain ensemble.

“And the second reason is because I really would have liked this outfit for myself,” Kris admitted. “And she kind of stole it.”

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians matriarch might feel free to judge her kids’ and Cait’s clothes, but she’d never tell her son-in-law Kanye West if she didn’t like his taste in fashion.
