DC Young Fly is an American internet sensation and Hollywood personality who became famous in the comedy series "Wild N' Out." Since achieving success in his career, many people have wondered whether DC Young Fly has a wife.

DC Young Fly, also known as John Whitfield, is not just an internet celebrity but also a TV star, rapper, and stand-up comedian. Given that he has over 11 million Instagram followers, it appears that he has a massive female fan base.

Regardless of how many female fans swoon over his charm and exceptional talent, DC Young Fly only has one woman in his life for whom he continues to swoon: Jacky Oh.

DC Young Fly and Jacky Oh at the State Farm Arena attending the game between the Denver Nuggets and the Atlanta Hawks on December 17, 2021, in Atlanta, Georgia. | Source: Getty Images

Jacky Oh Is Not Just DC Young Fly's Partner

Jacky has established herself as a model, businesswoman, actress, artist, producer, and content creator. The multitalented lady graduated from the University of California, Berkeley.

Jacky and DC Young Fly have been together since 2015.

Jacky Oh Is Just a Nickname

People have wondered whether or not the multitalented social media star's name is her real name, but in September of 2017, she set the record straight by stating that "Jacky" is her real name and "Oh" is just a moniker.

She explained where her catchy nickname came from, saying that someone in college used to call her "Jacky Oh," and she liked how it sounded to her ears. She has used the name ever since.

Some of Jacky's fans have also thought she is a white woman because of her unique features, but she clarified that she is biracial because she was born to a white mother and an African-American father.

She Grew up with a Single Father

Despite her mixed ethnicity, Jacky said she was raised entirely by her single father and was surrounded by her aunts, uncles, and cousins who are of African American descent. She admitted she was never around her mother's family.

Jacky had only a few recollections of her mother. She said that her mother used to visit frequently when she was younger, but as time progressed, they grew distant as her mother stopped seeing her.

As a result of her mother's absence, Jacky has formed a "special bond" with her father, with whom she has grown increasingly close. She considers her father her best friend because they can talk about anything.

Jacky is no stranger to being mistaken for white by the public. She pointed out the "issue" with social media, stating that netizens would tell her what she should and should not do because they believed she was not Black.

She also said some online users would persist in judging her, particularly in her dating life. People dislike seeing Black men and White women together, according to Jacky. She said: "But I'm Black, all of it shouldn't even matter."

Jacky Oh and DC Young Fly Met during the Filming of the Series

Jacky and DC Young Fly have been together since 2015. The couple first met in a hotel lobby where DC Young Fly was filming the first season of the "Wild N' Out" series hosted by Nick Cannon. Jacky said:

"We started dating right after the show wrapped. I flew back to California, he flew back to Atlanta. I think I flew to Atlanta the next week."

Even though Jacky and her boyfriend have been dating for several years, they have no plans to marry anytime soon. She added that their relationship has been great, and they are happy with their current situation.

Jacky Gave Birth to 2 Daughters from DC Young Fly

Jacky shares two daughters with DC Young Fly: Nova and Nala Whitfield. Jacky's eldest daughter, Nova, was born in October 2016, but despite her young age, she has already achieved great success.

Jacky felt overjoyed when she had her first child. Despite admitting that her hormones were all over the place after her daughter's birth, she never felt disconnected or sad for her child.

Nova is already following in her mother's footsteps. As of this writing, she has a massive following on Instagram and YouTube, with 178,000 followers and over 97,000 subscribers, respectively.

Nova's sister, Nala, was born in August 2020. Like her older sibling, Nala seems to be a rising star on social media and has frequently featured on her parents' and sister's online pages.
