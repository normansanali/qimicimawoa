Elizabeth Olsen and Anthony Mackie, who are both part of the Avengers, had the best reunion at the 2021 MTV Movie & TV Awards!

Elizabeth won Best Performance in a Show and Best Fight for WandaVision, which won Best Show. Anthony won Best Hero and Best Duo for The Falcon and the Winter Soldier. The Disney+ shows were the big winners of the night!
