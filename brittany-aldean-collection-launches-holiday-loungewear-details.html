Brittany Aldean Collection Launches Holiday Loungewear: Details

September 07, 2024
blog

Brittany Aldean has got your holiday PJ game all figured out! Stylish can exclusively reveal that the wife of country star Jason Aldean is launching her eponymous clothing line on Monday, November 19, and she is kicking things off with a festive (and baby bump-friendly!) loungewear collection that is perfect for cozying up by the fire all holiday season long.

“As a pregnant mom, I wanted to start my first clothing line with practical and comfy pieces that I can easily move around in while still looking cute with my family at the holidays,” the mom, who is currently expecting her second child, tells Us. “I can’t wait until we get to expand the line next year.”

While the Brittany Aldean Collection will ultimately include a full line of inclusive ready-to-wear clothing and accessories in 2019, she decided to start with the capsule of pajamas, sweatshirts, beanies and more because she’s all about keeping things comfy this time of year.

“When I was designing this line, comfort was one of the most important considerations. The holidays are for lying by the fire, wrapping presents and enjoying time with your family and friends,” she says. “All of these pieces are great for staying comfortable while still looking cute and stylish for those inevitable photos that will be taken!”
